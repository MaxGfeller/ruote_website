h2. ruote

<strong>Ruote</strong> is a workflow engine written in Ruby.  It's a runtime for long running [business] processes.

This website is about the ruote 0.9.x line, it's kept as an archive.

Development has moved on and is now at "ruote 2.1":http://ruote.rubyforge.org. Meet you there.
