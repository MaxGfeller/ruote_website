h2. source

Ruote's source code is located at the "github":http://github.com

* "ruote 2.0":http://github.com/jmettraux/ruote/tree/ruote2.0 - the workflow engine

* "ruote-dm":http://github.com/jmettraux/ruote-dm/ - "DataMapper" extensions for ruote (mostly DmParticipant)
* "ruote-activerecord":http://github.com/kennethkalmer/ruote-activerecord - ActiveRecord participants and co for ruote
* "ruote-amqp":http://github.com/asm/ruote-amqp/ - AMQP participants and listeners for ruote (coming soon for ruote 2.0) (by "Kenneth":http://opensourcery.co.za and "Jason":http://github.com/asm/)

* "ruote-fluo":http://github.com/jmettraux/ruote-fluo/ - some kind of a browser based editor for process definitions ("online demo":http://difference.openwfe.org:4567/?pdef=pdef.rb)

* "ruote-kit":http://github.com/kennethkalmer/ruote-kit/ - RESTful wrapper for ruote (by "Kenneth":http://opensourcery.co.za)
* "ruote-http":http://github.com/jmettraux/ruote-http/ - mini ruote-kit

See also "rufus":http://rufus.rubyforge.org, a set of ruby gems spun off of ruote.
