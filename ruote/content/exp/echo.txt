h2. echo

An expression for echoing text to STDOUT or to a :s_tracer service
(if there is one bound in the engine context).

<% coderay(:lang => 'ruby', :line_numbers => 'inline') do -%>
  sequence do
    participant :ref => 'toto'
    echo 'toto replied'
  end
<% end %>
