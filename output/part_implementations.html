participant implementations

participants and receivers

  • ruote-amqp – An AMQP participant/receiver pair and remote process launcher

participants found in ruote

  • block participant – a Ruby block as a ruote participant
  • Ruote::HashParticipant – storing workitems in memory (Ruby Hash)
  • Ruote::NoOpParticipant – a participant that immediately replies to the engine
  • Ruote::NullParticipant – a ‘/dev/null’ participant, never replies to the engine
  • Ruote::SmtpParticipant – a base for more elaborate email participants
  • Ruote::EngineParticipant – a bridge to launch [segments of] processes on other ruote engines
  • Ruote::StorageParticipant – storing the workitems in the engine storage

other participants

  • RuoteAmqp::Participant – places workitems in an AMQP queue (there is also an assorted listener)
  • Ruote::Jig::JigParticipant – a HTTP client participant
  • Rufus::Decision::Participant – this participat implementation is located in rufus-decision it wraps a decision table inside of a ruote participant
